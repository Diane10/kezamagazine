PROFESSIONAL APPEARANCE

Career Self Care
Sympathique Blogger
14 June 2022
5 mins Read

With the knowledge of a professional experts, their looks don’t matter that much. At least that is what we were led to believe. Nowadays people judge your looks before considering the qualifications you possess as a professional.

Speaking from personal experience, it is very hard for a health care provider to establish a friendly patient-nurse relationship while consulting the client. Looks can sometimes be deceiving. It can serve well the person under certain circumstances like to some people mostly ladies looking young can be a desirable thing because the younger they look, the more attractive they are perceived. Others wish to look older for the same purposes, validation in this category. The thing is there is not much we can change to the physical appearance of one’s height, face and physical figure.

People can change looks in gyms and hair styles and grooming but other parameters mentioned above come naturally. So, in the right mind, people shouldn’t judge physical aspect of a professional as long as they don’t serve in the job. Unless you are in the army force or other security companies or similar entities, you can be a professional of any body size. It isn’t a matter of capabilities or qualifications if you don’t look fit or seem capable in the eyes of the clients, you are out of favor.

The worst comes out when you are looking for a job and the hiring committees keep ignoring you without mentioning the cause and all of a sudden you hear a comment like “you are going to do better when you grow up!” that’s when you know that the adulthood in your mind doesn’t relate to your looks. It goes on to the extent that you have to show the identity card to prove that you are old enough to be doing that otherwise you are screwed.

To anyone engaged in this exchange of services and money, professional ethics and client’s rights have to be considered in pursuit of client satisfaction. A client very much prefers a clean and net service or product provider. Clients also have rights to choose where to get their products or services from. Therefor dress professionally, look smart not just for your own confidence but also for the peace of mind of a person searching for your services or products.

For the best you can change as far as your looks are concerned, do it for the consumers of whatever you supply. But if you to the other end, please respect the fact that people are there not for favor but for a qualification they possess. Hire people not because they can look better in the job but because they can perform the task very well, except for models which is both. Be considering, that might be your very best call in that decision making position.
